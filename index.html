Valentine's Day Pups

Pawlina, a well-known south Texpaws beagle, has given birth to an astonishing litter of 18 puppies on Saturday, February 14th. This beats the previous record of 12 puppies set by her mother, Pawsephone. Since these puppies were born on Valentine's Day, they are all named after Valentine's Day traditions.

Puppy Graduation

While other puppies were hard at work in their junior year of puppyhood, this puppy celebrated his early graduation from the Puppy Academy. Tater Tot, a golden retriever, was the first of his litter to graduate. The graduation ceremony was held in the Puppy Academy's main hall, where all the puppies' parents and teachers were in attendance.

Since Tater Tot graduated in the top 10% of his class, he was awarded a special graduation certificate and instant admission to a university of his choice. Tater Tot is now enrolled at the University of Texpaws at Dallas.
